Beer Can Races Tracker

The Race tracker

Welcome to the Chicago Beer Can Races Tracker

This tracker allows racers willing to share their data to visualize their races in a friendly user interface. More feature will come as we develop this website. For any suggestions feel free to contact us.

The functionalities are very basic for now, but depending on the interest of the users, we'll expand/automate things as the year goes on.

If you are interested in seeing your boat on this page, you need to get a GPS logger app on your phone. You need one that can send the log files via email at chicago.beercanracing@gmail.com in the GPX or NMEA format.

Apps needed to log your data

Any app that can log the GPS points and export to NMEA or GPX is fine. It is recomended to log at a frequency of 1 GPS position every 2-10 seconds. The data shown on the website will be sampled down before being represented on the page otherwise displaying data for many boats will be too slow.

Privacy considerations

By sending data, you are providing it to the whole community. It will be hosted on GitHub along with the webpages of this website. If you have reasons not to share, do not send any data.
